Service

PRESCHOOL and AFTERSCHOOL

We offer Preschool and Afterschool services to children from our community. We can accommodate up to 50 children at the time (we are constantly working to increase this number in order to meet a demand for our services) We run session from 7:30am to 6:30pm Monday to Sunday in our Preschool Junior and Preschool Senior rooms.We also gladly accommodate your child outside those hours in order to meet your needs.
CHILDCARE

Parents Rescue meets the expectations of all parents. It offers tailored services to the parents in need of professional help in caring for children at any time parents may require 24/7/365! This offer is addressed to anyone who needs childcare, for those who work, those who want to go on romantic weekend break, have to go to business metting and for those who just need to relax for a few hours. In Parents Rescue we have more than 100 trained,certified and experienced carers located throughout Ireland, ready to appear at the parents' home at any time. All staff are carefully vetted and will provide references on request.
RATIO

The maximum number of children which the Preschool Senior rooms can accommodate is 22 per session. The ratio is 11 children to 1 childcare practitioner. In Preschool Junior room it is 5 children to 1 childcare practitioner for children under 2.5 years old and 6 to 1 childcare practitioner for children over 2.5 years per session. It is safe,friendly and colorful childrens` complex, which is located in the vicinity ofspectacular Emo Court*, co. Laois. There are several play rooms for children (in which there are pre-school and afterschool classes for about 30 children from the area), a library, a tv/game room and bedrooms for children (also including toddler rooms). The building is located near small forest with several playgrounds and a garden. Plenty of fun-educational activities are organized so that none of the children is bored ... and they don't miss their parents too much :)
OUTDOOR PLAY AREA

The outdoor area is an essential asset to the service that provides an opportunity for children of all ages to develop gross motor skills in a safe and secure environment. We ask that children are suitably dressed to faciliate their involvement in the outdoor play are all year around. We have a multi purpose play ship, springers, sand, water, ride on toys, games, a large green area with beautiful garden, a playhouse and horticultural activities to ensure variety and that each child's ongoing needs are met.

We have an access to the private forest with lots of fascinating attractions hidden in it. Due to close proximity to spectacular Emo Court we like to take our children there for a pleasant walk.
ACTIVITIES

Activities are planned by a qualified Early Years Practitioner that incorporates the different stages of the child into a daily routine. All our team have been trained in the Highscope approach. All children have the opportunity to learn through play and engage in activities that are age and stage appropriate. These will be altered on a regular basis to ensure variety and that each child's ongoing needs are met.
