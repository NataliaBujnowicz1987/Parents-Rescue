About us

HISTORY

The Parent's Rescue has been in operation since 1996. We are registered, legal company, named The Parent's Rescue - Professional Childcare, Group Limited, We are located in a beutiful place in Emo, co Laois. It was established by Mary and David Downey. They have 3 children and at the time they realised they can't find the childcare service that would suit their needs so the decided to open their own place! Owning a big country house they established pre and after-school service where children have few playrooms, library,TV room, kitchen as well as sleep room at their disposal. There is also a big garden with forestry access. Parents Rescue House is located in the vicinity of spectacular Emo Court, co. Laois. The gardens at Emo are 35 hectares of landscaped grounds, with formal areas, woodland walks,statues and a 20-acre lake – a feature of neoclassical landscape design. More about Emo Court CLICK HERE

From 2018 company started to provide nationwide nanny service (more information in SERVICE page). We are currently working with over a 100 of qualified staff. Out people are our biggest asset and are indeed a fundamental part of Parent Rescue family. We provide annual training refreshers free of charge for them.We also subsidise up to 50% of a cost of third level education qualification if they wish to obtain it.
OUR MISSION

The Parent's Rescue is working with parents and local community endaevours to provide quality, affordable childcare in response to the needs of parents and carers in the local community. Also, we endeavour to provide a safe and stimulating environment conductive to the enhancement of the well-being of all children.

Childhood is the foundation for later life and as such, the management and all Staff of The Parent's Rescue aspire to provide, first and foremost, a safe, happy fun and stimulating environment for the children of our community to grow and develop to the best of their individual abilities. We acknowledge that learning begins at an early age and we will accommodate this learning by promoting the holistic development of the individual child through age and stage appropriate programmes

We aspire to emphasise the importance of independence, self-discipline, self-confidance, curiosity, creativity, problem-solving, co-operation and respect for others and self; there by helping them develop competent, considerate, responsible members of society.
